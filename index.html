Genomic signatures of adaptation in response to environmental stressors

Jordan JM, Smith A, Garcia R, Williams T
Journal of Molecular Biology (2025), 42(3): 156-172
This study identifies key genomic signatures associated with adaptation to various environmental stressors in model organisms, providing insights into evolutionary mechanisms.

Computational framework for predicting evolutionary trajectories in microbial communities

Chen L, Jordan JM, Thompson K, Davis P
Bioinformatics (2024), 38(5): 423-435
We present a novel computational framework that integrates genomic data with environmental parameters to predict evolutionary trajectories in complex microbial communities.
