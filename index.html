Welcome to the Jordan Group

Ask me anything about our research, publications, or resources

Where Genomes Meet Algorithms

The Jordan Group fuses high-throughput molecular biology with deep-learning models to decode—and re-engineer—cellular metabolism.

Integrative Omics

We layer bulk & single-cell RNA-seq, ATAC-seq, ChIP-seq, and spatial proteomics to build a multi-resolution map of metabolic regulation.

AI-Driven Structure & Dynamics

Custom AlphaFold-Multimer + MD pipelines predict how protonation-sensitive histidine networks reshape protein interfaces.

Translational Systems Biology

From in silico design to CRISPR screens and live-cell metabolic analyzers, we close the loop between computation and bench.

A Single Pipeline, End-to-End

Our cloud-native stack ingests raw reads, trains graph neural networks, and pushes insights straight to automated wet-lab protocols—no copy-pasting required.

  • Neural LLM Agents orchestrate QC & analysis
  • Blackwell-class GPUs render protein movies in minutes
  • Slack/Notion bridges keep the whole lab in sync

Current Focus Areas

Histidine-Rich Interfaces as pH Switches

We test the idea that histidine clusters act as metabolic rheostats, tuning mitochondrial calcium uniporter activity in response to nutrient flux.

Chromatin Remodeling by Pioneer Factors

Combining Cut&Tag-seq with AlphaFold 3 molecular movies to watch cBAF pry open "closed" chromatin and license Pol II recruitment.

AI-Optimized Therapeutic Targets

A multimodal GNN prioritizes gene–drug pairs that reverse steatotic signatures in hepatocytes—validated in siRNA mini-screens.
